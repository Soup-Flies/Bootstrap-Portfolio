About

When I was in elementary school I went to a neighbors garage sale. I found a gift that I thought my dad would be interested in, a book on programming C++. While I never personally read the book, I always thought that I would enjoy coding of some sort. Fast forward to my late teens and a friend of mine turns me onto Linux. I start to play around in the terminal and hack together my 1st script to automatically download all of the webcomics I enjoyed reading so much. At one point I decided to compile my own kernel of Linux from scratch which I found to be quite the adventure. Another friend introduced Project Euler to me and I have been off and on playing with problems on there with Python. I lost touch with most forms of coding until recently at work I wrote a VBA script to automate some forms I need to fill out for our customers shipping information, and it was at that time I made the call to finally get into a coding bootcamp.
